Strawberry Long Island Iced Tea

Description

Get prepared for the ultimate drink that will knock your right off of your feet but please drink responsibly! The strawberry long island iced tea is a blend of several different alcohols combined and the curious part is that it doesn't actually contain tea at all. The strawberry offers a sweeter alternative over the traditional coke to combine with the multitude of flavors the alcohol provides.

Featuring Vodka, Tequila, Gin, Rum, and more, this superpowered drink is bound to spark excitement for a fun night!

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. (30ml) Tequila
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Vodka
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Rum
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Triple Sec
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Gin
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Sweet N Sour
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Strawberry Liqueur
  • Strawberry Slices
  • Lime Wheel

Steps

  1. 1. Combine all ingredients besides strawberry in an ice filled glass. Shake.
  2. Fill tall glass with ice and strawberries and add mix. Drizzle in strawberry liqueur.
  3. Garnish the lime wheel.
  4. Drink responsibly!
