Cheesecake

Description

The New York Cheesecake is a classic desert that is sure to send your tastebuds to another planet. With a thick graham cracker crust that is buttery and a little sweet, you are just beginning your cheesecake journey. The next stop is the delectable cream cheese filling which is topped with a delicious strawberry sauce.

Your mouth will be in heaven and thank you after making this exciting New York Cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Melted Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Heavy Cream
