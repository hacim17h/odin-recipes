Cheesecake

Description

The New York Cheesecake is a classic desert that is sure to send your taste buds to another planet. With a thick graham cracker crust that is buttery and a little sweet, you are just beginning your cheesecake journey. The next stop is the delectable cream cheese filling which is topped with a delicious strawberry sauce.

Your mouth will be in heaven and thank you after making this exciting New York Cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups graham crackers, pulsed into crumbs
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 7 tablespoons butter, melted

For the New York-Style Cheesecake:

  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, very soft
  • 1 cup Full-Fat Sour Cream
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs + 2 egg yolks, at room temperature
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

For the Fresh Strawberry Sauce:

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced, divided
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 small lemon, zested
