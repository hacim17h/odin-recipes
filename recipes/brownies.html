Brownies

Description

Brownies are the ultimate fudge-filled snack that are sure to hit all the right spots for you sweet fanatics. They feature a dense fudgy interior with a moist and rich texture that is sure to stimulate the senses. In addition to that, they are fresh and made from scratch with little difficulty so it's a great starter project for you and even the family.

Well without further ado, here's the ultimate brownie recipe you and your mouth as been waiting for!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Salt and vanilla
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with sugar, salt, and cocoa powder.
  2. Add the vanilla and eggs.
  3. Add the flour.
  4. Bake the brownies in the oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
